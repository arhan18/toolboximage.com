## Why Crop Images?

### 1. Improve Composition
The rule of thirds, leading lines, and removing distracting elements all start with a good crop.

### 2. Standardize for Platforms
Each social platform has its own preferred aspect ratio. Cropping ensures your image fits perfectly without awkward white bars or cut-off subjects.

### 3. Focus on the Subject
Remove unnecessary background and direct the viewer's attention exactly where you want it.

### 4. Meet Submission Requirements
Passport photos, visa applications, university ID cards, and professional certifications all require specific image dimensions and crops.

## Why Pixel-Perfect Cropping Matters

Most photo editing apps on phones and simple online tools round crop dimensions to the nearest convenient value. This works for casual use, but if you're cropping images for:

- **Print** — off-by-2 pixels at 300 DPI equals 0.17 mm on paper, which is noticeable on precision documents
- **E-commerce** — inconsistent image sizes hurt your store's professional appearance
- **Social media ads** — Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok have exact dimension requirements; being off by even 4 pixels can trigger automatic re-cropping

The ToolBox Image Cropper maintains exact pixel dimensions throughout the process. What you see is exactly what you get.
